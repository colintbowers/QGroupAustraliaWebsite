Membership

Membership of the Q Group is open to finance practitioners and researchers with an interest in quantitative techniques in finance and investment.

Who can become a member?

The Q Group welcomes individuals working in or studying areas where quantitative methods play a central role in finance and investment. This includes professionals in banking, asset management, superannuation, insurance, risk management, regulation and financial technology, as well as academics and students.

Non‑members are welcome to browse the public sections of this site and to attend selected public events. Some events and services are reserved for financial members.

How to enquire about membership

If you are interested in joining the Q Group, please send us an application or enquiry using the contact details below. The committee will respond with further information about membership and next steps.
