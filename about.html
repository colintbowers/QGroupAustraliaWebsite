About us

The Q Group is an association of Australian finance practitioners and researchers who are active in the development and use of quantitative techniques in finance and investment.

Who we are

The Institute of Quantitative Research in Finance Inc. (Q Group) is a not‑for‑profit association that brings together professionals and academics interested in quantitative approaches to finance and investment. Our members include practitioners from banks, asset managers, superannuation funds, regulators and consulting firms, as well as researchers from universities and other institutions.

The primary objective of the Q Group is to help our members and the wider community to understand, apply and develop quantitative solutions to financial and investment problems. We do this by providing a forum for the sharing of ideas, research and practical experience.

What we do

Q Group activities span a range of formats designed to encourage interaction between practitioners and researchers:

  • Regular seminars, workshops and lunch series on topics such as risk management, portfolio construction, derivatives pricing and superannuation.
  • Collaborative events with universities and industry partners, including finance symposia and colloquia.
  • Support for specialist initiatives such as the Sydney Financial Mathematics Workshop (SFMW).

Events are generally targeted at quantitative finance professionals but are often open to the broader investment community, students and academics with an interest in applied quantitative methods.

Who should join

Membership is open to individuals with an interest in quantitative finance, whether as industry practitioners, academics, students or regulators. Non‑members are welcome to browse the public sections of this site and to attend selected public events.

If you are interested in becoming a member, please see the membership page or contact the Q Group committee.
