The Institute of Quantitative Research in Finance Inc.

Q Group

The Q Group is an association of Australian finance practitioners and researchers who are active in the development and use of quantitative techniques in finance and investment.

Our primary objective is to help our members and the wider community to understand, apply and develop quantitative solutions to financial and investment problems.

About the Q Group

This website is primarily intended as a service to our members. However, non-members are welcome to browse the public sections of this site. If you are interested in joining the Q Group, please send us an application using the contact details below.

Public Information

The Q Group supports the development and dissemination of quantitative techniques in finance and investment. From time to time, public information and resources are made available here for practitioners, academics and students with an interest in quantitative finance.
