Recent Q Group events

A selection of recent seminars, symposia and member events organised by the Q Group across Australia.

Q Group AGM

Date: 7 Dec 2020 Location: Online

Usually at this time of year it is the committee's great pleasure to invite you to our annual Sydney Christmas dinner and AGM. However, 2020 differed somewhat from the norm, so members were invited to the first virtual AGM on Monday, 7 December at 7pm (AEDT).

All financial members were welcome.

RMIT–Q Group–CFA Society Melbourne Finance Symposium

Date: 28 Aug 2020 Location: Online

A half‑day virtual symposium presented by RMIT, the Q Group and CFA Society Melbourne. The Finance Symposium 2020 brought together RMIT’s top finance academics and industry leaders to explore and discuss the implications of COVID‑19 and the way forward for financial markets and the economy.

2020 Monash–Q Group Colloquium – POSTPONED

Date: 27 Mar 2020 Location: Monash Conference Centre, 30 Collins St, Melbourne

Jointly organised by Monash Business School, the Monash Centre for Quantitative Finance and Investment Strategies and Q Group, the colloquium focuses on banking, quantitative finance, investment strategies and innovation in the industry.

Q Group Sydney Monthly Lunch Series – The ‘Right’ Level for the Superannuation Guarantee

Date: 24 Mar 2020 Speaker: Dr Geoff Warren Location: RedOak Beer Cafe, Sydney

Part of the Sydney Monthly Lunch Series, this session examined the “right” level for the Superannuation Guarantee (SG). Geoff presented quantitative work showing how different assumptions can lead to different optimal SG levels and discussed the policy debate around superannuation.

Q Group Melbourne Seminar – International SD Factors and Covariance Risk

Date: 20 Feb 2020 Speaker: Dr Michael Herold, Bamber University Location: 360 Collins St, Melbourne

A seminar jointly organised by Q Group and La Trobe University on international stochastic discount factors and covariance risk in portfolio construction and asset pricing.

Sydney Monthly Lunch Series – Metrics and Methods for Hedging Left Tail Risk

Date: 20 Feb 2020 Speaker: Associate Professor James Doran Location: RedOak Beer Cafe, Sydney

A lunch seminar on metrics and methods for hedging left‑tail risk in investment portfolios, drawing on both academic theory and practitioner experience.

Q Group Melbourne Christmas Lunch

Date: 6 Dec 2019 Location: Bamboo House, Melbourne

An end‑of‑year lunch for Q Group members in Melbourne. The event was free for 2019 financial members, with opportunities to reconnect with colleagues and celebrate the year in quantitative finance.

Sydney Christmas Dinner and AGM

Date: 3 Dec 2019 Location: Prime Steak Restaurant, Sydney GPO

The committee’s annual Sydney Christmas dinner and AGM, with a three‑course dinner for financial members and Colin’s world‑famous quant trivia quiz following the formal business of the AGM.
